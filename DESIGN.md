# Overview

The Strengths Writer admin surface takes its cues from the login screen: sharp edges, warm parchment backgrounds, and deep evergreen accents. The visual tone is calm and editorial while still feeling structured and deliberate. The interface should feel carved, not soft.

**Key characteristics**

- Cream canvas (#f7f2ea) with evergreen (#1f3d39) as the primary anchor.
- White surfaces for cards and panels, with subtle warm beige borders.
- Typography is bold and editorial; uppercase micro-labels stay crisp.
- Buttons, cards, and panels are square-cornered; no pill shapes for primary controls.
- Shadows are soft and deep, used sparingly to separate key layers.

## Colors

### Brand & Accent

- **Evergreen** (#1f3d39): Primary actions, headers, and anchor accents.
- **Evergreen Soft** (#2b776a): Success states and secondary highlight.
- **Parchment** (#f7f2ea): Primary background and contrast color for dark accents.

### Surfaces

- **Canvas** (#f7f2ea): Default page background.
- **Surface** (#ffffff): Cards, tables, and content panels.
- **Surface Soft** (#efe7dc): Hover panels and soft separators.
- **Hairline** (#e3ddd3): Borders on cards, tables, and inputs.

### Text

- **Heading** (#1f3d39): Page titles and key labels.
- **Body** (#3a332f): Primary paragraph copy.
- **Muted** (#7b6f64): Metadata, captions, and labels.

### Semantic

- **Success** (#2b776a): Approval and positive statuses.
- **Danger** (#b4534a): Destructive actions.
- **Info** (#3a6b8f): Informational badges.

## Typography

- **Primary font**: Inter (weights 400 / 600 / 700).
- **Mono**: JetBrains Mono for metadata, slugs, and system labels.
- **Uppercase labels**: 11-12px, heavy weight, wide tracking (0.14em to 0.2em).

## Layout

- **Grid**: 12-column layout, max width ~1280px.
- **Section rhythm**: 40-48px between primary dashboard bands.
- **Card padding**: 24-32px depending on density.
- **Editor workspace**: Multi-pane layout with firm vertical dividers.

## Shapes

- **Sharp edges**: Cards, buttons, and panels are square-cornered.
- **Minimal rounding**: Only tiny status dots and avatars may be circular.

## Components

### Admin Header

- Canvas background with a subtle hairline divider.
- Navigation links use muted text and switch to evergreen on active state.

### Cards & Panels

- White surfaces with hairline borders.
- No radius; rely on contrast and spacing for separation.

### Buttons

- Primary buttons: evergreen background, parchment text.
- Secondary buttons: white background, evergreen text, hairline border.
- No rounding; keep padding consistent with login forms.

### Inputs

- Soft beige background (#fbfaf6) with hairline borders.
- Strong focus ring in evergreen.

### Status Labels

- Flat rectangles with uppercase text.
- Use semantic colors with subtle tints.
